Who this is for

This pathway is for people who want a structured and realistic approach rather than generic exercises. The aim is to identify what is limiting you and progress safely.

  • Recurring lower back pain
  • Morning stiffness or desk-related pain
  • Fear of bending, lifting or exercise

Session structure

  1. Assessment and goal setting
  2. Movement testing and clinical reasoning
  3. Guided corrective exercise
  4. Progression plan and home routine
